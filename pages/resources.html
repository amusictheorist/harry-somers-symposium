Discography: Major Recording Releases

Harry Somers Anthology of Canadian Music: Radio Canada International ACM–7, recorded from 1947 to 1973 and released as a 10–LP box set in 1980. Includes Scherzo for Strings (1947); Rhapsody for violin and piano (1948); North Country (1948); Suite for Harp and Chamber Orchestra (1949); String Quartet No. 2 (1950); The Fool (1953); Violin Sonata No. 1 (1953); Passacaglia and Fugue for orchestra (1954); Violin Sonata No. 2 (1955); Where Do We Stand, O Lord? (1955); Two Songs for the Coming of Spring (1955); Five Songs for Dark Voice (1956); Fantasia for Orchestra (1958); String Quartet No. 3 (1959); Symphony for Woodwinds, Brass, and Percussion (1961); Gloria (1962); Twelve Miniatures (1963); The Wonder Song (1964); Evocations (1966); Five Songs of the Newfoundland Outports (1969); Voiceplay (1971); Music for Solo Violin (1973).

Sacred and Profane Somers, Centrediscs CMCCD 5495, 1995; includes Five Songs of the Newfoundland Outports (1969); Kyrie (1972); Three Limericks (1980).

The Spring of Somers, CBC SMCD5162, 1996; includes North Country (1948); Suite for Harp and Chamber Orchestra (1949); Symphony No. 1 (1951).

Harry Somers Celebration, CBC SMCD 5199, 2000; includes Five Songs for Dark Voice (1956); Elegy, Transformation, Jubilation (1981); Third Piano Concerto (1996).

Canadian Composers Portraits: Harry Somers, Centrediscs CMCCD 11306 (4 CDs), 2006; includes audio documentary by Eitan Cornfield plus North Country (1948); Suite for harp and chamber orchestra (1949); Symphony No. 1 (1951); Five Songs for Dark Voice (1956); Lyric for orchestra (1960); Picasso Suite (1964); Elegy, Transformation, Jubilation (1981); Concertante for violin, percussion and string orchestra (1982); Of Memory and Desire (1993); Third Piano Concerto (1996).
